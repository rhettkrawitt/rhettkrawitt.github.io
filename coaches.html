Redwood Sailing Team

9th in the state, Best team north of Santa Barbara, Thanks to our Amazing Coaches

Head Coach Dan Brandt

Dan Brandt is an incredible coach and sailor. He has experience coaching a wide range of boats, including 29ers, Laser 2s, FJs, C420s, RS21s, and countless others. He also has lots of experience sailing, owning his wooden keelboat, and sailing on anything from lasers to Aussie 18s.

Dan has been coaching the Redwood Sailing Team for 2 years and is also the coach for many of the team's club sailors. He has worked tirelessly to help the Redwood Sailing Team become what it is today, and we are grateful for all his work.

Assistant Coach Melissa Purdy

Melissa Purdy is a former Olympian and mother of another sailor on the team. She has proved invaluable to the team in her first season, coaching during practices and providing free coaching for many local regattas. Without her continual support, the team would not have seen the success it saw at the local NorCal regattas this season.

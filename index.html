Redwood High School Sailing Team is a very competitive team, competing against many other high schools across the West Coast and California, in addition to occasional national competitions. Made up of 16 sailors and coached by Head Coach Dan Brandt and Assistant Coach Melissa Purdy, the team is training and improving in hopes of qualifying for nationals next year. A developing team with many freshman and sophmores, the team is very excited for the future events and is looking forward to improving and growing into a top national team.

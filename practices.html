Redwood Sailing Team: Practices

Schedule

The Redwood Sailing Team practiced every Tuesday and Wednesday after school until sunset during the 2023-2024 Season. The team takes the months of December and January off due to light winds and minimal light. For the 2024-2025 season, the team plans to switch to Wednesdays and Thursdays for practice in hopes to line up better with school schedules

Typical Practice

  1. Right After School -- Arrive
  2. Rig the Boats
  3. Team Meeting
  4. Change
  5. Go Sailing
  6. Do Drills
  7. Sail Home
  8. De-Rig
  9. Team Meeting to De-Breif
  10. Sunset -- Go Home

Drills We Do

  • Tacking/Gybing on the Whistle
  • Cone/Corridor Drill
  • Continuous Rabbit Starts
  • Rolling Starts
  • Practice Races
  • Mystery Starts
  • Team Racing
  • Match Racing
